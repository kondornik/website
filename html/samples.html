SenseBot

Search Engine that finds sense in a heap of Web pages

Here you can browse sample summaries that will give you a better idea of where SenseBot can be applicable. This page is updated on an ongoing basis.

The sample queries are loosely grouped into vertical search areas, although many queries would fit into more than one area. The criteria for the quality of the summaries is broad, but generally consists of one or more of the following qualifications:

  • the summary gives a good idea of the topic of the query;
  • the summary gives a better idea of the topic of the query than the first page of results of the search engine (e.g., Google);
  • the summary identifies sources which are rich in content relevant to the topic and can be further explored in depth;
  • the summary is readable and coherent.
